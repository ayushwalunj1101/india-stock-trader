## Notes

1. **This list is approximate.** Nifty 200 reconstitutes semi-annually. Some stocks may have been added/removed in the latest review. When in doubt, verify against NSE's official list.
2. **Midcap 100 is the high-alpha hunting ground.** These stocks have less analyst coverage, more room for price discovery, but also higher slippage and gap risk. Always check `references/india-mechanics.md` before trading midcaps.
3. **Sector groupings are for scanning convenience.** Some stocks appear in multiple sectors (e.g., VEDL in both Metals and Midcap). Use the grouping that matches the current market theme.
4. **Total count may vary slightly** from exactly 200 due to timing of reconstitution, mergers, or delistings.
